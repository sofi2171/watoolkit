Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 01, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how WA Toolkit ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Data Collection & Local Storage

We highly respect your privacy. WA Toolkit is designed to function locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal messages, contacts, or media on our servers.

  • Status Saver: Media files (images/videos) downloaded via the app are stored strictly on your device's local storage.
  • Deleted Messages: To provide this feature, the app requires NotificationListenerService access. Messages captured from notifications are saved entirely in your phone's local database. We have no access to them.
  • Direct Chat: Phone numbers entered for direct messaging are only saved in your local app history for your convenience and are never uploaded online.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements in our app to keep it free for users. AdMob is an advertising service provided by Google Inc.

Google AdMob & Cookies: Google uses tracking technologies like cookies and device Advertising IDs (such as Android Advertising ID) to serve personalized ads based on your past visits to our app or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing behavior.

Opting Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting your Android device settings: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization. For more details on how Google uses your data, please visit the Google Privacy & Terms page.

3. Analytics & Crash Reports

We may use third-party tools (like Google Analytics for Firebase) to collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your device model, operating system version, and crash logs. This helps us fix bugs and improve app performance. This data does not contain any of your personal chat data.

4. App Permissions

Our app requires certain permissions to function correctly:

  • Storage / Manage External Storage: Required to fetch and save WhatsApp statuses to your gallery.
  • Notification Access: Required specifically for the "Deleted Messages" tool to read incoming notifications safely in the background.
